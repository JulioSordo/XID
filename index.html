In a world divided by borders and fragmented systems, XID emerges as a new way to exist: a sovereign, universal digital identity built on the XRP Ledger. XID is your story, your health, your education, your economy, your voice, and your freedom‚Äîwithout intermediaries, exclusions, or the possibility of falsification.

From a child born in a remote village to an engineer building cities on the Moon, everyone deserves an identity that represents, protects, and connects them to a fairer world. XID is your Life Charter‚Äîbecause in the future, sovereignty is programmed, not granted.

Modules of the XID Life Charter

Health

Medical history, vaccines, insurance, and instant access with privacy control.

Finances

Multi-currency wallet on XRPL, payments, credit, and global transactions.

Education

Verifiable NFTs for diplomas and certificates from institutions.

Licenses

Digital permits (driving, operating, etc.) with secure authentication.

Voting

Decentralized, fraud-free democratic participation.

Global Citizenship

Rights and existence recognition independent of any state.

Technical Architecture

  • Built on XRP Ledger: Fast validation, low cost, high scalability.
  • Smart Contracts for modules: Health, finances, education, licenses, and voting.
  • Cryptographic Security: Biometric signatures and end-to-end encryption.
  • Interoperability: REST APIs and mobile SDKs for external integration.
  • Resilience: Decentralized network with globally replicated nodes.

Roadmap

  • Q2 2025: Manifesto release and GitHub repository launch.
  • Q3 2025: Alpha version of the XID wallet.
  • Q4 2025: Pilot testing of 'Modo Vida' with healthcare partners.
  • Q1 2026: Public beta with health and finance modules.
  • Q2 2026: Integration of licenses and voting module.
  • Q3 2026: Partnerships with international organizations for global citizenship.
